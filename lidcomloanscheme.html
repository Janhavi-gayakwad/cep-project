LIDCOM Margin Money Loan Scheme


Being implemented particularly for the (Scheduled Caste) Charmakar Community, the "Margin Money Loan Scheme" is a scheme by the Leather Industries Development Corporation (LIDCOM), Govt. of Maharashtra.

Benefit

1.For a loan limit from ₹50,001 to ₹5,00,000/-, 20% of the Project Cost Loan as Seed Capital is paid by the Corporation @ 4% interest per annum.
2. A maximum of ₹10,000/- will be paid as a subsidy by Corporation.
3. A 5% amount of project cost will have to be contributed by the beneficiary as his share.
4.Balance 75% of the project cost will be paid by Bank at the existing rate.
5.The said amount of the loan is to be refunded within 36 to 60 monthly installments.

Eligibility

1.The applicant should be a citizen of India.
2.The applicant should be a permanent resident of Maharashtra State.
3.The applicant should be in the 18 to 50 years age group.
4.The applicant should be from Charmakar Community only (Dhor, Chambhar, Holar, Mochi, etc.).
5.The annual income of the family of the applicant should be less than or equal to ₹1,00,000/-.
6. applicant should have knowledge of the business for which he has applied for the loan.


Documents Required

1.Passport-size Photo.
2.Aadhaar Card.
4.Identity Card of Maharashtra Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board.
5.Bank Passbook.
6.Marksheet/School Certificate of passing in the previous academic level.
7.Acknowledgment/Bonafide for admission in the current academic year.
8.Proof of Residence.


Application Process:

Step 01: Take the format of the application form from the District Office of LIDCOM
Step 02:Fill in all the mandatory fields, paste the passport-sized photograph (signed across), and attach all the (self-attested) mandatory documents.
Step 03:Submit the duly filled and signed application form along with the documents to the District Office.
Step 04:Submit the duly filled and signed application form along with the documents to the District Office.
