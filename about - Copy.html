About Us

About Sevatric

Welcome to Sevatric, your trusted resource for the latest information on government schemes and welfare programs in Maharashtra. Our mission is to empower residents of Maharashtra by providing easy access to essential details about various state and central government initiatives designed to improve quality of life, foster economic growth, and support social welfare.

Our Focus

We aim to bridge the information gap and help you stay informed about:

  • Agricultural Schemes: Assistance programs for farmers, including subsidies, loans, crop insurance, and training initiatives to boost agricultural productivity.
  • Education and Skill Development: Scholarships, vocational training, and skill development programs aimed at enhancing educational and employment opportunities for students and young professionals.
  • Healthcare Initiatives: Health insurance schemes, maternity and child welfare programs, and other health benefits provided to residents.
  • Employment and Entrepreneurship: Job guarantee schemes, small business loans, and startup assistance programs for Maharashtra’s youth and entrepreneurs.
  • Social Welfare Programs: Support programs for women, elderly citizens, differently-abled individuals, and those from economically weaker sections of society.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create an accessible platform where you can find comprehensive information on all relevant schemes, eligibility criteria, benefits, and application processes. We strive to keep this information accurate, up-to-date, and easy to understand.
